About Us

Lomasney Motors is a well established family business founded in 1987, with 33 years experience, we have an excellent reputation for quality service at affordable rates, no job is too big or small.

We are an all makes car and van service and repair garage ,where you can expect to achieve great savings compared to main dealer prices.We do the same working using the latest technology and quality products.We serve local busineses and private customers.

Call in and see us whenever you like, free tea and coffee while you wait!

Meet the Team

Dermot Lomasney

Dermot owns and runs the business and is a qualified mechanic with 50 years experience. He set up his own business in 1987 and built it from the ground up. It started with one customer coming in to get their car serviced and from there the customer base has grown exponentially. Dermot says "The garage is part of my life, dealing with people is my favourite part of the job. The business feels part of the community."

Cillian McCarthy

Cillian is our lead mechanic and joined the team in 2007 at 17 years of age . Previous to this Cillian would spend hes days after school with us as he always had a keen interest in the automotive industry .13 years later he is now a fully qualified mechanic since 2012 with a level 6 advanced motor mechanic qualification .
