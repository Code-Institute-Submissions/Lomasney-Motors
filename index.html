About Us

Lomasney Motors is a well established family business founded in 1987, with 33 years experience, we have an excellent reputation for quality service at affordable rates, no job is too big or small.

We are an all makes car and van service and repair garage ,where you can expect to achieve great savings compared to main dealer prices.We do the same working using the latest technology and quality products.We serve local busineses and private customers.

Call in and see us whenever you like, free tea and coffee while you wait!

Meet the Team

Dermot Lomasney

Dermot is the owner of Lomasney Motors and has been in the mechanical services business for 50 years. He started his own business in 1987 that prides itself on it's high quality professional standards and friendly environment. Dermot has a vast amount of industry experience that has accounted for his success
