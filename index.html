About Us

Lomasney Motors is a well established family business founded in 1987, with 33 years experience, we have an excellent reputation for quality service at affordable rates, no job is too big or small. We are an all makes car and van service and repair garage ,where you can expect to achieve great savings compared to main dealer prices.We do the same working using the latest technology and quality products.We serve local busineses and private customers. Call in and see us whenever you like, free tea and coffee while you wait!
